Sample Email

Dear Representative,



My name is [blank] and I am writing you to express my opinion on the issue of the carbon tax. I believe that the carbon tax is a prudent measure to not only improve the environment for the United States, but also to take steps towards energy self-dependence. I hope that you can support legislation implementing this policy in the future, and doing so would make me much more likely to support your future campaigns.

Climate change is a pressing issue for the US and the rest of the world. In 2016 at the Paris Conference on climate change President Obama agreed to cut US carbon emissions. To meet this goal, we need measures beyond burdensome executive regulation. The Supreme Court decision of February, making it more difficult for the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) to impose new carbon emission regulations, makes alternatives to traditional controls more necessary than ever.

A vast majority of economists believe that the carbon tax is the most efficient way to reduce carbon emissions. The carbon tax avoids the inefficiencies and redundancies that often plague regulatory agencies and the heavy costs associated with subsidies. Rather, it is a simple, yet powerful, market-based reform that empowers American businesses to make healthy changes in their business practices.

The Congressional Budget Office (CBO) estimates that a $20 tax on every metric ton of gas emissions would reduce US carbon emissions by 8% over 9 years. This is a tremendous change in carbon emissions in the United States that would mitigate climate change. Moreover, it would provide compelling incentives for businesses to utilize more sustainable energy resources that will make the United States and its businesses ready for a future powered by renewable energy rather than fossil fuels.


Sincerely,
[Name]
